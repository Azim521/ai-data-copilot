=== KEY METRIC DEFINITIONS ===

GMV (Gross Merchandise Value): The total value of goods sold before deducting returns or discounts. GMV = unit_price × quantity. This is the top-line growth metric.

Revenue: Actual realized income after applying discounts. Revenue = GMV × (1 - discount_pct/100). This is the bottom-line sales figure.

Return Rate: Percentage of orders where return_flag = 1. Target return rate is below 10%. Anything above 15% is a red flag.

Discount Rate: Average discount_pct across orders. Company policy caps discounts at 20% for Standard segment and 25% for Premium segment.

=== CUSTOMER SEGMENTS ===

Premium: High-value customers. Average order value above 1500. Churn risk if rating drops below 3.
Standard: Core customer base. Average order value 800-1500.
Budget: Price-sensitive customers. Primarily active during discount campaigns.

=== CHANNEL DEFINITIONS ===

App: Mobile application orders. Fastest growing channel. Target: 50% of total orders by end of 2024.
Website: Desktop/web orders. Mature channel. Stable growth expected.
In-Store: Physical retail orders. Declining channel. Under review for consolidation.

=== CATEGORY BENCHMARKS ===

Electronics: Highest GMV per order (avg 2500). Seasonal spike in Q4 (Diwali, Christmas).
Apparel: Second highest volume. High return rate (avg 18%) — acceptable for category.
Grocery: Lowest GMV per order but highest order frequency. Low returns expected (<5%).
Beauty: Growing category. Target 15% YoY GMV growth.
Home & Kitchen: Stable. Mostly Standard and Premium segments.
Sports: Seasonal — peaks in Q1 (New Year resolutions) and Q3 (monsoon end).
Books: Lowest GMV. Mostly Budget segment. Low returns.
Toys: Q4 seasonal spike (35% of annual GMV in Q4).

=== DATA QUALITY NOTES ===

unit_price: May have ~3% missing values due to legacy system migration.
rating: Customer ratings 1-5. Missing means customer did not submit a review.
revenue: Occasionally missing for B2B bulk orders processed offline.

=== ANOMALY THRESHOLDS ===

GMV per order above 10,000 should be flagged for manual review (potential data entry error or wholesale order).
discount_pct above 25% is unauthorized and should be investigated.
return_flag = 1 with rating = 5 is suspicious — flag for fraud review.
